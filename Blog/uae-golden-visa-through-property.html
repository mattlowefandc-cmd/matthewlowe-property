How to Get a UAE Golden Visa Through Property

By Matthew Lowe · March 2026 · 8 min read

The UAE Golden Visa is the most valuable residency programme available to property investors. Here is the exact step-by-step process I guide my UK clients through.

Requirements

  • Property worth AED 2 million+ (approx £430,000)
  • Property must be in a freehold area
  • Can be residential or commercial
  • Multiple properties can be combined to reach the threshold
  • Valid passport with minimum 6 months validity
  • Health insurance valid in the UAE

The 7-Step Process

  1. Purchase qualifying property and obtain title deed from DLD
  2. Request a no-objection certificate from DLD
  3. Apply online through ICP (Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship)
  4. Upload documents: passport, title deed, property valuation, photos
  5. Entry permit issued (if applying from outside UAE)
  6. Medical fitness test and Emirates ID registration in UAE
  7. Golden Visa stamped in passport — valid for 10 years

Timeline

Once property is purchased and title deed obtained: 2–4 weeks for complete visa processing.

Costs

  • Application fee: AED 2,800–3,800
  • Medical test: AED 300–500
  • Emirates ID: AED 370
  • Health insurance: AED 3,000–8,000/year
  • Total: approximately AED 7,000–13,000 (£1,500–£2,800)
