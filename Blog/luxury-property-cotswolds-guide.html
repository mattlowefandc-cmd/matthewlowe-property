Luxury Property in the Cotswolds

By Matthew Lowe · March 2026 · 9 min read

The Cotswolds is England's most coveted countryside location. Rolling hills, honey-stone villages, and some of the finest country estates in the world — it's where London's elite retreat and where international buyers dream of owning.

Why the Cotswolds?

  • 90 minutes from London — close enough for weekends, far enough for peace
  • Outstanding natural beauty — an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty (AONB)
  • World-class schools — including Cheltenham Ladies' College and Kingham Hill
  • Celebrity neighbours — the Beckhams, Jeremy Clarkson, Kate Moss
  • Strong capital appreciation — limited supply drives consistent growth

Best Villages for Luxury Buyers

  • Chipping Campden: Quintessential Cotswolds. Grade I listed High Street. Properties from £800K to £5M+
  • Burford: "The Gateway to the Cotswolds." Excellent pubs, antique shops, stunning architecture
  • Stow-on-the-Wold: Historic market town with premium period properties
  • Cirencester: The "Capital of the Cotswolds." Larger town with excellent amenities
  • Broadway: Picture-perfect village with some of the finest country houses in England

Price Guide

  • Village cottage (3-bed): £500,000–£1,200,000
  • Family house (4-5 bed): £1,000,000–£3,000,000
  • Country estate (6+ bed): £3,000,000–£15,000,000+
  • Historic manor house: £5,000,000–£25,000,000+
