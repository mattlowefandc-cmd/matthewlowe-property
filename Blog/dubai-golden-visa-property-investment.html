Dubai Golden Visa Through Property Investment

By Matthew Lowe · March 2026 · 10 min read

The UAE Golden Visa is one of the most powerful residency programmes available to international investors. For UK buyers purchasing property in Dubai, it offers 10-year renewable residency with extraordinary benefits.

What Is the UAE Golden Visa?

Launched in 2019 and expanded significantly in 2022, the Golden Visa is a long-term residency visa that allows foreign nationals to live, work, and study in the UAE without the need for a national sponsor. For property investors, this is a game-changer.

Eligibility Through Property

To qualify for the 10-year Golden Visa through property investment:

  • Purchase property worth a minimum of AED 2 million (approximately £430,000)
  • The property must be in a freehold area
  • It can be one or multiple properties totalling AED 2 million
  • The property can be mortgaged — only the equity paid needs to meet the threshold
  • Off-plan properties from approved developers may qualify

Benefits of the Golden Visa

  • 10-year residency — renewable indefinitely
  • Sponsor family members — spouse, children, and domestic staff
  • No minimum stay requirement — you don't need to live in the UAE
  • Open a UAE bank account — essential for property management
  • Obtain a UAE driving licence
  • Access UAE healthcare and education
  • Tax residency certificate — potentially reduce your global tax liability (consult a tax advisor)

Key Advantage for UK Investors

The Golden Visa doesn't require you to give up your British citizenship or residency. You can hold both simultaneously, giving you the freedom to split time between the UK and Dubai as you choose.

Application Process

  1. Purchase qualifying property — AED 2M+ in a freehold area
  2. Obtain title deed — Registered with Dubai Land Department
  3. Get a property valuation letter — From DLD confirming ownership value
  4. Apply via ICP or GDRFA — Online application with supporting documents
  5. Medical fitness test — Standard health check in the UAE
  6. Emirates ID — Biometric registration
  7. Visa stamped — Typically 2–4 weeks total processing

Which Properties Qualify?

Almost any freehold property meeting the AED 2M threshold qualifies. Popular choices for UK investors include:

  • Palm Jumeirah apartments: Starting from AED 2.5M
  • Dubai Marina 2-beds: From AED 2M
  • Downtown Dubai 1-beds: Premium units from AED 2M
  • Dubai Hills villas: From AED 3M
