Why UK Investors Are Buying in Dubai

By Matthew Lowe · March 2026 · 7 min read

I've watched the trend accelerate dramatically over the past three years. More British high-net-worth investors are allocating capital to Dubai than ever before. Here's why — and whether you should too.

The 7 Reasons

1. Tax Efficiency

Zero CGT, zero income tax on rent, zero inheritance tax. For a higher-rate UK taxpayer, the savings on a £2M property generating £120K annual rent are approximately £54,000 per year.

2. Superior Yields

6–9% net vs 2–3% in London. The mathematics are impossible to ignore.

3. The Golden Visa

10-year UAE residency with a single property purchase. Banking access, family sponsorship, and potential tax residency benefits.

4. Lifestyle

Year-round sunshine, world-class infrastructure, safety, and a direct 7-hour flight from London. Many investors end up spending 3–4 months per year in Dubai.

5. Portfolio Diversification

Exposure to a different economy, currency (AED pegged to USD), and property cycle reduces overall portfolio risk.

6. Developer Quality

New-build properties in Dubai are finished to a standard that UK new-builds rarely match — at a fraction of the price.

7. Market Momentum

Dubai's population is forecast to reach 5.8 million by 2040 (from 3.6M today). More people = more demand = sustained price growth.
