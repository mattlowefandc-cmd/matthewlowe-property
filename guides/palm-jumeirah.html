Palm Jumeirah Area Guide

Investment & Lifestyle Guide · March 2026

The Palm Jumeirah is a global architectural icon and Dubai's premier destination for ultra-high-net-worth individuals. If you are looking for beachfront luxury, exclusivity, and status, this is it.

The Market Dynamics

The Palm is split into the Trunk (mostly apartments) and the Fronds (Signature Villas, Garden Homes, and Custom Mansions). The Fronds offer private beach access, making them some of the most illiquid and highly prized assets in the city.

Property Types and Prices (2026)

  • Trunk Apartments (1-2 beds): AED 2M – AED 6M
  • Luxury Penthouses (Atlantis The Royal, One Palm): AED 30M – AED 150M+
  • Garden Homes (Fronds): AED 18M – AED 35M
  • Signature Villas & Custom Mansions (Fronds): AED 40M – AED 300M+

Why Buy on the Palm?

Supply on the Palm is fundamentally capped. No new fronds can be built, meaning demand for private beach villas consistently outstrips supply, driving long-term appreciation.
