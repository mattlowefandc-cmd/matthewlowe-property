Prime Central London Area Guide

Investment & Lifestyle Guide · March 2026

Prime Central London (PCL) is one of the world's most resilient and established property markets. Acting as a global safe haven for wealth, it encompasses the exclusive enclaves of Mayfair, Belgravia, Knightsbridge, and Kensington.

The Micro-Markets

  • Mayfair: The absolute pinnacle. Dominated by historic townhouses, luxury boutiques, and private members' clubs. Prices regularly exceed £4,000 per sq ft.
  • Belgravia: Grand white-stucco terraces and quiet garden squares. Highly favoured by international embassies and ultra-HNW families.
  • Knightsbridge: Close to Harrods and Hyde Park. Excellent for pied-à-terre apartments and international investors.
  • Kensington: A blend of diplomatic residences, royal palaces, and grand family homes.

Investment Outlook 2026

PCL remains highly attractive to dollar-pegged investors (including those from the UAE). With prices still presenting value compared to previous peaks, 2026 offers strategic buying opportunities for long-term capital preservation.
