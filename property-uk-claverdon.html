Property Overview

Nestled within the prestigious and highly sought-after Little Aston Park Estate, this spectacular five-bedroom family home combines luxury living with elegant design, offering a unique blend of comfort and style.

The residence features meticulously landscaped gardens and a secluded setting that provides a perfect retreat. The heart of the home is the expansive Kitchen/Breakfast Room with Family Area, featuring bespoke hand-painted cabinetry, Corian countertops, and high-end integrated Neff appliances. Bi-folding doors open onto the patio, blending indoor and outdoor living spaces seamlessly.

Completing the ground floor is a fully equipped private gym and a grand reception hall with elegant panelled walls and polished ceramic flooring.

Strategic Market Analysis

Little Aston Park is widely regarded as one of the West Midlands' most prestigious private estates, offering a level of security and exclusivity that continues to command a premium. Properties here benefit from a unique 'micro-market' that remains historically insulated from regional economic fluctuation.

For the luxury homeowner or investor, this residence represents a high-specification asset in a location where replacement value and demand for turnkey family homes consistently outstrip supply. The proximity to Sutton Park and elite local schooling further reinforces the long-term capital preservation potential of this estate.
